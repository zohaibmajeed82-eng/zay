About Me

Hello! I’m Zohaib, a creative director and visual storyteller who believes that the most impactful design comes from a balance of artistic intuition and technical precision.

As the founder of ZAY and Vice President of the Aks UET Photography Society, I specialize in bringing brand identities to life through high-end corporate photography and purposeful, detail-oriented design.

My approach to creative direction is uniquely shaped by my background as a final-year Metallurgical and Materials Engineering student at UET Lahore. I bring an engineer’s rigorous eye for detail and structure to the creative process.

Whether I am managing the broad vision of a corporate shoot or fine-tuning the subtle, micro-details of a design, my goal is always to craft polished, compelling visual narratives that leave a lasting impression.
